ABOUT US

The Goodphellas is an artist driven collective/label based in Southern California. Founded by Noah Phillips (aka Mosaic) and Rance Dvorak (aka RANCE) in November of 2018, the collective officially launched with its debut EP in January of 2019. Since then it has been steadily growing, with collective releases receiving support from well-known artists such as Hekler and Sweet Teeth. We aim to feature an eclectic catalogue of music ranging multiple festival genres, and also seek to help the underground as much as possible. The Goodphellas are all about good people and good music.
